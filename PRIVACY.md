# Privacy Policy for Sticky Notes Chrome Extension

**Last updated: April 2026**

Sticky Notes is a Chrome browser extension that lets you create virtual sticky notes on any webpage. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**Sticky Notes does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All note data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. No data is sent to any server, API, or third-party service.

## Permissions

- **`storage`** — Used to save your notes locally on your device. No data leaves your browser.
- **`downloads`** — Used to export your notes as a JSON backup file. The file is saved to your local Downloads folder.
- **`contextMenus`** — Adds a right-click menu option to save selected text as a sticky note. No data is transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

Sticky Notes does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Export & Deletion

- You can export all your notes at any time using the Export feature in the dashboard.
- Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data from your device.
- Chrome's "Clear browsing data" feature can also remove extension storage.

## Changes

If this policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected here with an updated date.
